About Me

I'm a doctoral researcher at École Nationale Supérieure d'Arts et Métiers (ENSAM) in Metz, France, collaborating with ArcelorMittal Maizières Research. My PhD focuses on shape optimization applied to fluid-structure coupling in the steel continuous casting process.

My journey spans three countries — a B.Tech at VIT University in India, an MSc at Khalifa University in the UAE, and my current PhD in France. I integrate computational fluid dynamics with AI/ML frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-learn to accelerate engineering optimization.
